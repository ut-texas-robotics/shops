Wood Shop (AHG 1.102)

Wood Shop Access

The AHG Analog Fabrication Lab (Wood Shop) is available to users in AHG. To get access to the shop, users should contact the lab manager Henry Smith (henry.smith@austin.utexas.edu) to schedule a time to be trained in the shop. Note that the wood shop is only available when a staff member is present. To see the AHG Wood Shop hours, view the calendar here: Wood Shop Calendar

To schedule a training session: Wood Shop Training

Equipment

There is a variety of woodworking equipment in the Wood Shop as can be seen in the photos below.

Equipment includes various sanders, drill press, bandsaws, chop saw, table saw, panel saw, and more. For more information, please reach out to the lab manager Henry Smith and attend a Wood Shop training session.
