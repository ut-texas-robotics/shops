DigiFab Shop (AHG 1.106)

The DigiFab shop (AHG 1.106) contains many resources for quick digital fabrication including 3D printers, a large laser cutter, and a CAD/CAM workstation.

All of the resources in the DigiFab shop require training before users are provided access. If you have not been trained on a machine, contact a shop manager or the building manager to request training for the machine(s) you are interested in. All machines (except for the Epilog Laser cutter) are available for users 24/7. The Epilog laser is available Monday-Friday 9am-5pm.

Remember to schedule your 3D prints on the Google Calendar! (Raise3D, FormLabs, and Markforged Printers)

Prusa MK3S+ FDM 3D Printers

Smaller desktop 3D printers with easily removable flexible heatbed making part removal effortless. 25x21x21cm build volume, 0.05 to 0.35mm layer height options, and support for most standard 3D printing filament materials.

No reservation needed, DigiFab lab houses 4 Prusa MK3S+ printers allowing for many users to be able to prototype parts quickly and with minimal hassle.

Prusa MK3S+ User Guide

Raise3D FDM 3D Printers

Raise3D Pro2Plus and Pro3 printers are high precision, dual-extruder, large scale printers. The Pro2Plus has a build volume of 30x30x60cm, and the Pro3 has a build volume of 30x30x30cm.

Uses Raise3D branded filament only. Great for larger prints that may not fit on the smaller printers in the lab. Also highly reliable for important time sensitive tasks.

Raise3D User Guide

FormLabs SLA 3D Printers

The FormLabs printers (Form3 and Form3BL) allow for high fidelity prints with a wide variety of resin materials including flexible, rigid, clear, ESD safe, materials and many more.

See all of the FormLabs Materials

FormLabs User Guide

Epilog Fusion Pro Laser Cutter

The Epilog Fusion Pro Laser Cutter allows for the cutting and engraving of various materials including acrylic, birch plywood, leather, and more. The laser has a large 48"x36" print area allowing for large sheets of material to be placed on it.
